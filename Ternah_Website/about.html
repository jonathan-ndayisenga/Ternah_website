About Ternah

More than code.
A digital partner.

TERNAH Software Company Ltd is an African software development and digital transformation company building innovative, reliable, and scalable technology for businesses, institutions, and communities.

Our story

Software, crafted to fit.

We started Ternah on a simple conviction: technology should fit the way you already work, not force you to work the way it wants.

We combine technical depth, business understanding, and local insight to create software that solves real problems across Africa. We help organizations embrace digital transformation, improve efficiency, and unlock new opportunities, and we stay for the long term.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading software company, driving digital transformation through innovative, reliable, locally relevant technology.

Our Mission

To empower businesses, institutions, and communities across Africa with affordable, scalable, impactful digital solutions, fostering innovation and indigenous technology.
